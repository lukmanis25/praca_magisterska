Question:
How does Gdańsk University of Technology define the process and limitations of confirming learning outcomes for admission?

Answer:
**Confirming Learning Outcomes for Admission at Gdańsk University of Technology**

According to the provided information, Gdańsk University of Technology defines the process and limitations of confirming learning outcomes for admission as follows:

*   **Procedure for Admission by Transfer**: The university conducts proceedings for admission by transfer from another university through a committee appointed by a person authorized by the rector.
*   **Confirmation of Learning Outcomes**: Learning outcomes are confirmed in the scope corresponding to the learning outcomes specified in the curriculum. As a result of confirming the learning outcomes, no more than 50% of ECTS credits assigned to the classes included in the study program may be credited.
*   **Order of Admission**: The order of admission is determined by the result of learning outcome confirmation.
*   **Tutor Appointment**: The dean may appoint a tutor for the student accepted for studies as a result of confirming the learning outcomes.
*   **Detailed Rules**: The detailed rules regarding confirmation of learning outcomes at Gdańsk University of Technology are set out in separate regulations.
