Question:
What training components are mandatory for students during their first semester at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
**Mandatory Training Components for First Semester Students**

According to the provided information, during their first semester at Gdańsk University of Technology, students are required to undergo mandatory training in the following components:

* Health and safety
* Library training
* Study rules
* Use of the 'Moja PG' system
* Preparation for participation in classes conducted using distance learning methods and techniques

These training components aim to ensure that students are well-equipped to navigate the university's policies, facilities, and technology, as well as to prepare them for their academic studies.
