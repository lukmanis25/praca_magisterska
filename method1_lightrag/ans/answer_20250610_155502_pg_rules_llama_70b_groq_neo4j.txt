Question:
Under what circumstances can a student request an examination to be conducted before a committee, and what is the composition of such a committee?

Answer:
**Request for Examination by Committee**
=====================================

According to ยง14, a student can request an examination to be conducted before a committee in the following circumstances:

*   Irregularities in the conduct or marking process of the original exam.
*   The scope of the examination exceeds the scope specified in the subject card.
*   The academic teacher did not comply with the deadlines specified in ยง13(6), (7) and (8) and in ยง14(4).

Additionally, ยง6 states that a student, a representative of the Student Government, or the examiner can submit a request to conduct an examination before a committee within 14 days of the announcement of the original exam results, presenting a detailed justification of the charges.

**Composition of the Examination Committee**
-----------------------------------------

The examination committee consists of:

*   The dean as chairperson.
*   The examiner who conducted the examination in question.
*   A specialist appointed by the dean in the subject matter of the exam or a related subject.
*   A specialist indicated by the student in the subject matter of the examination or a related subject.

At the request of the student, the examination committee can also be joined by a representative of the Student Government.
