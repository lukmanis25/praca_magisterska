Question:
According to the text, under what circumstances may a diploma thesis be submitted in the form of a published or accepted article, and what additional requirement must be met in such cases?

Answer:
**Submission of Diploma Thesis in the Form of a Published or Accepted Article**

According to the text, a diploma thesis may be submitted in the form of a published or accepted article under the following circumstances:

* The student's request, approved by the diploma thesis supervisor, is approved by the dean.
* The article must be published or accepted for publication in a peer-reviewed journal or post-conference materials.
* The article must be related to learning outcomes achieved in a given field, level, and profile of studies.
* The article must be in accordance with the current ministerial list for a minimum of 100 points.
* The student must be the first author if the article is multi-authored.

Additionally, in such cases, the student is required to submit a self-report.
