Question:
What are the specific conditions under which a student may be removed from a class, and how is such an absence classified?

Answer:
**Removal from the Student Register due to Absence**
=====================================================

According to the provided regulations, a student may be removed from the student register due to absence from compulsory classes in the following circumstances:

* Unjustified absence from 4 classes as part of the same form of classes in the selected subject covered by the study plan (Paragraph 7).

**Classification of Absence**
-------------------------

The absence is classified as unjustified, implying that the student's reasons for missing classes are not acceptable according to the university's regulations.

**Note**
----

The regulations do not specify the consequences of justified absence from classes. It is possible that the regulations provide for a different classification or consequences for justified absence, but this information is not provided in the given text.
