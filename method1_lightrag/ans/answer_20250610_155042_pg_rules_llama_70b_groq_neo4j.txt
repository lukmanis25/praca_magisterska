# Mandatory Training Components for First Semester Students
## Overview
The rules specify the mandatory training components for students during their first semester at Gdańsk University of Technology.

## Mandatory Components
According to the provided rules, the following components are mandatory for students during their first semester:

* Confirmation of learning outcomes
* Registration for the semester

These components are crucial for students to ensure they are properly admitted to studies and have access to the necessary resources and classes.

## Additional Requirements
While not explicitly stated as mandatory, the rules imply that students may need to attend classes included in the curriculum of the given semester, as part of the registration process.

## Conclusion
In summary, students during their first semester at Gdańsk University of Technology must undergo confirmation of learning outcomes and register for the semester. These components are essential for a smooth start to their academic journey.
