Question:
What are the specific conditions that must be jointly fulfilled for a diploma thesis to be considered successfully submitted at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
To be considered successfully submitted, a diploma thesis must fulfill the following conditions jointly:

1. **The student submits a declaration of independent completion of the diploma thesis**.
2. **The diploma thesis is uploaded with attachments to the university repository of written diploma theses using the “MojaPG”, university system**.
3. **The thesis supervisor approves the thesis and sends it for review**.

These conditions are specified in the university procedure nr 3, as outlined in the source document.
