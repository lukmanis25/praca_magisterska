Question:
What training components are mandatory for students during their first semester at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
Mandatory training components for students during their first semester at Gdańsk University of Technology include:

* Health and safety training
* Library training
* Study rules training
* Use of the 'Moja PG' system training
* Preparation for participation in classes conducted using distance learning methods and techniques

These training components are designed to ensure that students are well-equipped to navigate the university's policies and procedures, as well as to effectively utilize the university's resources.
