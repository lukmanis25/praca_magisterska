Question:
Under what circumstances can a student request an examination to be conducted before a committee, and what is the composition of such a committee?

Answer:
**Requesting an Examination Before a Committee**
=====================================================

According to §14 of the provided document, a student can request an examination to be conducted before a committee in the following circumstances:

*   During the original exam, there were irregularities in its conduct or in the principles of its marking process.
*   The scope of the examination carried out exceeds the scope specified in the subject card.
*   The academic teacher did not comply with the deadlines specified in §13(6), (7) and (8) and in §14(4).

Additionally, a student can request a committee examination within 14 days of the announcement of the original exam results, presenting a detailed justification of the charges.

**Composition of the Examination Committee**
=============================================

The examination committee, referred to in paragraph 5, consists of:

*   The dean as chairperson
*   The examiner who conducted the examination in question
*   A specialist appointed by the dean in the subject matter of the exam or a related subject
*   A specialist indicated by the student in the subject matter of the examination or a related subject

At the request of the student, the examination committee can be joined by a representative of the Student Government.
