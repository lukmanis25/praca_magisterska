Question:
What are the obligations a student must fulfill after receiving approval for a transfer from another university to Gdańsk University of Technology?\

Answer:
**Transfer Obligations at Gdańsk University of Technology**

After receiving approval for a transfer from another university to Gdańsk University of Technology, a student must fulfill the following obligations:

### Taking the Oath
The student must take the oath defined in the Statute of Gdańsk University of Technology.

### Formal Confirmation
Formal confirmation of taking the oath takes place on the student's individual account in the Moja PG system.

### Obtaining a Student ID Card
The student has the right to obtain a student ID card.

### Completion of Mandatory Training
During the first semester of studies at Gdańsk University of Technology, students undergo mandatory training, including:

* Health and safety
* Library training
* Study rules
* The use of the ‘Moja PG’ system
* Preparation for participation in classes conducted using distance learning methods and techniques

### Signing the Contract
The student must sign the contract referred to in §9(1)(4) of these rules.

### Undergoing Training Courses
The student must undergo training courses referred to in §3(6).

### Other Obligations
The student's rights and obligations expire on the day of graduation or deletion from the student register; the person who has completed bachelor’s degree studies preserves the student rights until October 31 of the year in which they graduated, excluding the right to financial assistance.
