Question:
What rights and opportunities are granted to exceptionally gifted secondary school students who attend university classes at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
# Exceptionally Gifted Students at Gdańsk University of Technology

## Conditions and Mode of Study

According to the rules defined in the provided document, exceptionally gifted students are granted special conditions and mode of study at Gdańsk University of Technology.

## Conditions and Mode of Study for Exceptionally Gifted Students

The rules specify that exceptionally gifted students are allowed to pursue individual study plans and curricula adapted to their scientific interests or current situation. This means that they can have a more personalized approach to their education, which can be beneficial for their intellectual and creative development.

## Opportunities for Exceptionally Gifted Students

The rules also mention that exceptionally gifted students have the opportunity to participate in individual research studies, which can involve the implementation of scientific research as part of research projects. This can provide them with valuable experience and skills in research and development.
