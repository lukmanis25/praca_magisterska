Question:
What are the specific conditions that must be jointly fulfilled for a diploma thesis to be considered successfully submitted at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
**Conditions for Successful Submission of Diploma Thesis**
===========================================================

According to the provided document, a diploma thesis is considered to be successfully submitted when the following conditions are fulfilled jointly:

### 1. Declaration of Independent Completion
The student submits a declaration of independent completion of the diploma thesis.

### 2. Uploading of Thesis to University Repository
The diploma thesis is uploaded with attachments to the university repository of written diploma theses using the “MojaPG”, university system.

### 3. Approval by Thesis Supervisor
The thesis supervisor approves the thesis and sends it for review.

These conditions must be jointly fulfilled for a diploma thesis to be considered successfully submitted at Gdańsk University of Technology.
