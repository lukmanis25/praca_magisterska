Question:
What are the specific conditions under which a student may be removed from a class, and how is such an absence classified?

Answer:
**Student Removal from Class**
================================

According to the provided regulations, a student may be removed from the student register due to absence from compulsory classes under the following conditions:

### Unjustified Absence

*   The student must be absent from 4 classes as part of the same form of classes in the selected subject covered by the study plan.
*   The absence is unjustified, implying that the student did not provide a valid reason for their absence.

### Resignation

*   The student submits a declaration of resignation from studies in the Moja PG system.
*   The student fails to submit a registration application, as provided for in ยง28(9).

### Lack of Progress in Studies

*   Students who have been registered and fail to complete the same semester three times may be excluded from the student register on account of lack of progress in their studies.
