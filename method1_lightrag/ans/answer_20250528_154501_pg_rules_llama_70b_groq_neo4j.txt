Question:
What responsibilities do students have regarding their personal information and the communication channels used for study-related matters at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
**Personal Information and Communication Channels**

Students at Gdańsk University of Technology have several responsibilities regarding their personal information and the communication channels used for study-related matters.

### Personal Information

According to the university's rules, students are responsible for ensuring the accuracy and completeness of their personal information, including their contact details and academic records. Students must notify the university of any changes to their personal information in a timely manner.

### Communication Channels

Students are expected to regularly check their university email accounts and the Moja PG electronic system for important announcements, notifications, and study-related information. Students are also responsible for responding promptly to emails and messages from university staff and administrators.

In addition, students are required to take an oath upon admission, which includes a commitment to behave in accordance with the university's regulations and rules. This oath also includes a promise to respect the privacy and confidentiality of others, including university staff and students.
