Question:
What are the specific conditions under which a student may be removed from a class, and how is such an absence classified?

Answer:
According to the provided Knowledge Base, a student may be removed from a class due to unjustified absence from 4 classes as part of the same form of classes in the selected subject covered by the study plan (Source: [KG] id "1", content paragraph 7).

Regarding the classification of such an absence, it is stated that removal from the student register due to absence from compulsory classes occurs in the event of unjustified absence from 4 classes as part of the same form of classes in the selected subject covered by the study plan (Source: [KG] id "1", content paragraph 7).

Please note that the exact conditions and classification may vary depending on the specific circumstances and the university's policies, which are not fully detailed in the provided Knowledge Base.
