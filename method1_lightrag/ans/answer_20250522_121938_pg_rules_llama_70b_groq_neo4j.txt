Question:
What is the procedure and timeline for a student to apply for a transfer from another university to Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
To apply for a transfer from another university to Gdańsk University of Technology, a student should follow the procedure outlined below.

**Step 1: Submission of Transfer Application**

A student who is applying for a change of university submits a transfer application not later than one month before the beginning of the subsequent semester. The application should be submitted to the relevant authorities at Gdańsk University of Technology.

**Step 2: Approval of Transfer**

Following the transfer approval, a student is obliged to:

*   Take an oath, pursuant to the Statute of Gdańsk University of Technology.
*   Sign the contract referred to in §9(1)(4) of these rules.
*   Undergo training courses referred to in §3(6).

**Timeline**

The academic year starts on 1 October and ends on 30 September. It comprises two semesters (winter and summer), and includes a series of classes which end with basic examination and retake sessions, followed by internships and holidays.
