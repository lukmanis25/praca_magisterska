Question:
What types of training are students required to complete during the first semester of studies at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
According to the provided Knowledge Base, during the first semester of studies at Gdańsk University of Technology, students are required to undergo mandatory training, which includes:

* Health and safety training
* Library training
* Study rules training
* Training on the use of the 'Moja PG' system
* Preparation for participation in classes conducted using distance learning methods and techniques.

These trainings are aimed at ensuring that students are well-equipped with the necessary knowledge and skills to navigate the university's systems and procedures, as well as to prepare them for their academic journey.
