Question:
What responsibilities do students have regarding their personal information and the communication channels used for study-related matters at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
**Student Responsibilities Regarding Personal Information and Communication Channels**

At Gdańsk University of Technology, students have several responsibilities regarding their personal information and the communication channels used for study-related matters.

**Personal Information**

*   Students are responsible for maintaining the accuracy and completeness of their personal information, including their contact details and study plans.
*   Students must inform the university of any changes to their personal information in a timely manner.
*   Students are responsible for ensuring that their personal information is kept confidential and not shared with unauthorized parties.

**Communication Channels**

*   Students are responsible for regularly checking their university email accounts and other communication channels for important announcements and notifications.
*   Students must respond to messages and notifications from the university in a timely manner.
*   Students are responsible for using the university's communication channels, such as the Moja PG system, to access their study plans, grades, and other study-related information.
*   Students are responsible for keeping their university account and password confidential and not sharing them with unauthorized parties.
