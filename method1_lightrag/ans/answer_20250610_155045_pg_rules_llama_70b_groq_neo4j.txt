Question:
What responsibilities do students have regarding their personal information and the communication channels used for study-related matters at Gdańsk University of Technology?

Answer:
**Student Responsibilities at Gdańsk University of Technology**
===========================================================

According to the provided document, students have the following responsibilities regarding their personal information and communication channels:

### Personal Information

* Students are not explicitly mentioned to have specific responsibilities regarding their personal information. However, it is implied that students are expected to provide accurate and up-to-date information to the university.

### Communication Channels

* Students are responsible for checking their individual student accounts in the ‘Moja PG’ system for information about changes made in the study program (§10, point 20).
* Students are also responsible for submitting applications on electronic forms available in the Moja PG system (§10, point 21).

### General Responsibility

* Students are expected to comply with the rules and regulations of Gdańsk University of Technology, including those related to communication and personal information.
