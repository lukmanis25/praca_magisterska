Question:
According to the text, under what circumstances may a diploma thesis be submitted in the form of a published or accepted article, and what additional requirement must be met in such cases?

Answer:
According to the text, a diploma thesis may be submitted in the form of a published or accepted article under the following circumstances:

* The student's request, approved by the diploma thesis supervisor, is submitted to the dean.
* The article is published or accepted for publication in a peer-reviewed journal or post-conference materials (in accordance with the current ministerial list for a minimum of 100 points).
* The article is related to learning outcomes achieved in a given field, level, and profile of studies.
* The student is required to submit a self-report.

In such cases, the student must be the first author of the article.
