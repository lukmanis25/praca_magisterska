Question:
What are the obligations a student must fulfill after receiving approval for a transfer from another university to Gdańsk University of Technology?\

Answer:
After receiving approval for a transfer from another university to Gdańsk University of Technology, a student must fulfill the following obligations:

* Take an oath, pursuant to the Statute of Gdańsk University of Technology
* Sign the contract referred to in §9(1)(4) of these rules
* Undergo training courses referred to in §3(6)

These obligations are mentioned in the rules for transfer from another university to Gdańsk University of Technology, as stated in the document "Source 1".
