Question:
What procedural requirements must be fulfilled for the approval and implementation of individual research studies, including interdisciplinary ones?

Answer:
**Approval and Implementation of Individual Research Studies**

According to the provided document, the program and plan of individual research studies, including individual interdisciplinary studies, are determined by the student and their academic supervisor, pursuant to paragraph 4, and approved by the dean.

This means that the following procedural requirements must be fulfilled:

* The student and their academic supervisor determine the program and plan of the individual research studies, including interdisciplinary ones.
* The program and plan are approved by the dean.

**Reference to Relevant Document Section**

This information can be found in section 11 of the provided document.
